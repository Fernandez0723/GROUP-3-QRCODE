Week 13

Understanding the Self – Eastern & Western Perspectives

In this lesson, we explore the concept of the self from two perspectives:

Eastern Self: Interdependent, family-centered, harmonious, and socially connected.
Western Self: Independent, autonomous, focused on self-expression and individuality.

Use this week to reflect on how your culture shapes your self-identity and personal interactions with others. Activities include:

  • Compare examples of collectivism vs individualism in daily life.
  • Analyze case studies of social roles in different cultures.
  • Create your personal reflection chart of Eastern vs Western self traits.
